How it goes :
El pollo loco is a jump and run game. Fight your way through a horde of chickens to the final boss by collecting the bottles on the way and then throwing them at your opponents. Collect all ten coins to win. When you win the game, "Game Over" will appear on the screen.

Have fun !
That is how it goes :
